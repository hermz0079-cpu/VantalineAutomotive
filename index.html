About Us

Welcome to Vantaline Automotive, an independent car dealership proudly based in Wembley since 2023. Privately owned and operated, we’re committed to offering a friendly, transparent and stress‑free car‑ buying experience. Operating from our well‑presented unit with a forecourt, we stock a range of quality used vehicles to suit every budget and lifestyle. Customer satisfaction is at the heart of what we do – that’s why we offer free home delivery within 10 miles, and nationwide delivery at just £80 plus £1.20 per mile up to 80 miles beyond. Whether you visit us in person or shop from home, you can count on honest advice, great value and exceptional service from start to finish.
